HRE - Aims/Goals

We are a small Irish family run business that specializes in the tuning of Classic Mini and derivatives. We are based in the South west of Dublin and are in Business 15 years, over this time we have gathered a welt of knowledge. Our aim is to build a constant unwavering support structure with our customers. We are a low volume company to allow us to maintain this relationship. Give Paul a call or drop him a message any time and he will endeavor to answer you questions.

Mission

HRE is dedicated to design and building engine and transmission packages that win races and last seasons
Engine Builds and Race Car Preparation. Part of 'Team Eire' Classic Mini Racing Team.
